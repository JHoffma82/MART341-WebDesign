Introduction

This site is a very rudimentary guide to navigating government resources to hopefully encourage people to consider homsteading. It would have multiple social, psycholofical, environmental, and health benefits that may have positive rippling effects in modern society.

Available Funding

  • Grants: Free funding for specific projects such as organic farming or renewable energy use.
  • Loans: Low-interest loans for purchasing land, equipment, or infrastructure.
  • Tax Incentives: Benefits for sustainable farming practices or renewable energy use.

How to Apply

  1. Research available funding options through government websites like the USDA Farm Service Agency.
  2. Collect necessary documents for application process such as a business plan, proof of income, and identification.
  3. Complete the application forms and ensure they align with eligibility criteria.
  4. Submit the application before the deadline and follow up with the agency.
